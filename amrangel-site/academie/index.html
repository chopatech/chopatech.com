Académie

Learn the trade, not just the trend.

Short courses and a full diploma track, taught by the same pattern-cutters and machinists who run our floor.

Curriculum

Six tracks, beginner to diploma.

Every course runs in our working atelier — you're learning on the same machines used for live collections.

Pattern Cutting Fundamentals

Beginner

Start with flat-pattern basics: drafting a bodice block, sleeve, and skirt from body measurements, then learn to true seams and grade between sizes.

8 wks Duration2x/wk Sessions$340 Tuition

Industrial Machine Operation

Beginner

Hands-on training on lockstitch, overlock, and coverstitch machines — safe operation, tension troubleshooting, and seam finishing standards.

6 wks Duration2x/wk Sessions$280 Tuition

Draping & Silhouette

Intermediate

Work directly on the dress form to develop silhouette through draping rather than flat-pattern math — for bias cuts, asymmetry, and sculptural shapes.

10 wks Duration2x/wk Sessions$420 Tuition

Natural Formulation Lab

Intermediate

Formulate your own skin and hair products: ratios, preservation basics, and how to read an ingredient for what it actually does.

6 wks Duration1x/wk Sessions$310 Tuition

Collection Development

Advanced

Design, develop, and produce a small original collection from concept board to finished samples, presented at a year-end showcase.

14 wks Duration3x/wk Sessions$890 Tuition

Diploma in Fashion Design

Full-Time

Our complete two-year track combining every course above, plus business fundamentals, portfolio development, and an industry placement term.
