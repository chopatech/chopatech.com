Research Papers Feed

Landmark and recent papers shaping the future of AI and computer science — with direct links to original sources.

LLM / TRANSFORMERS
Attention Is All You Need
Vaswani, Shazeer, Parmar et al. — Google Brain
NeurIPS 2017 · 100,000+ citations
Introduced the Transformer — the architecture powering every modern LLM. Replaced RNNs with pure multi-head self-attention, enabling massive parallelization and unlimited scaling. Changed everything.
2017 · The paper that changed everythingREAD ON arXiv
LLM
GPT-4 Technical Report
OpenAI Research Team
OpenAI Technical Report, 2023
Introduces GPT-4 achieving human-level performance on the bar exam, SAT, GRE, and AP exams. First multimodal frontier model available at consumer scale. Sparked the global AI revolution.
2023 · GPT-4 foundationREAD ON arXiv
SCIENCE AI
Highly Accurate Protein Structure Prediction with AlphaFold
Jumper, Evans, Pritzel et al. — DeepMind
Nature, 2021 · Nobel Prize Category
Solved biology's 50-year grand challenge — predicting 3D protein structures from sequences with atomic accuracy. Predicted ~200M protein structures, transforming drug discovery and medicine forever.
2021 · Nobel Prize categoryREAD ON Nature
REASONING
DeepSeek-R1: Incentivizing Reasoning via Reinforcement Learning
DeepSeek-AI Research Team
arXiv, January 2025
Pure RL approach to developing reasoning without supervised fine-tuning. DeepSeek-R1 matches OpenAI o1 on math and coding benchmarks at a fraction of training cost. Disrupted global AI economics.
2025 · The economics disruptorREAD ON arXiv
VISION AI
An Image is Worth 16x16 Words: Transformers for Image Recognition (ViT)
Dosovitskiy, Beyer et al. — Google Brain
ICLR 2021
Applied Transformer attention to image patches, challenging CNN dominance. Foundation for DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, GPT-4V, Gemini, and every modern multimodal AI system on Earth.
2021 · Vision AI foundationREAD ON arXiv
AI ALIGNMENT
Constitutional AI: Harmlessness from AI Feedback
Bai, Jones, Ndousse et al. — Anthropic
arXiv, 2022 · Foundation of Claude
AI models critique their own outputs using written principles — enabling scalable alignment without massive human feedback. The foundational paper behind Anthropic's Claude family of models.
2022 · Claude's foundationREAD ON arXiv
ROBOTICS AI
RT-2: Vision-Language-Action Models Transfer Web Knowledge to Robotics
Brohan, Brown et al. — Google DeepMind
CoRL 2023
Vision-language models trained on web data applied directly to robotic control. Robots reason about novel tasks from text descriptions — the dawn of general-purpose physical AI systems.
2023 · Physical AI dawnREAD ON arXiv
QUANTUM
Quantum Supremacy Using a Programmable Superconducting Processor
Arute, Arya et al. — Google Quantum AI
Nature, 2019
Google's Sycamore computed in 200 seconds what a classical supercomputer would need ~10,000 years for — first experimental proof of quantum computational supremacy over classical computing.
2019 · Quantum supremacy provenREAD ON Nature
REINFORCEMENT LEARNING
Mastering the Game of Go with Deep Neural Networks and Tree Search
Silver, Huang, Maddison et al. — DeepMind
Nature, 2016 · AlphaGo
AlphaGo — first AI to defeat a world champion Go player. Deep RL + Monte Carlo tree search conquered a task believed impossible for machines, proving AI could master intuition-based reasoning.
2016 · AI mastered intuitionREAD ON Nature
