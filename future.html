Research Papers Feed

Landmark and recent papers shaping the future of AI and computer science — with direct links to original sources.

LLM
Attention Is All You Need
Vaswani, Shazeer, Parmar et al. — Google Brain
NeurIPS 2017
Introduced the Transformer — the architecture powering every modern LLM. Replaced RNNs with pure multi-head self-attention, enabling massive parallelization and scaling.
2017 · 100K+ citationsarXiv
LLM
GPT-4 Technical Report
OpenAI Research Team
OpenAI Technical Report 2023
Introduces GPT-4, achieving human-level performance on numerous professional benchmarks including the bar exam. First multimodal frontier model available at consumer scale.
2023 · GPT-4 foundationarXiv
SCIENCE AI
AlphaFold: Protein Structure Prediction
Jumper, Evans, Pritzel et al. — DeepMind
Nature, 2021
Solved one of biology's greatest challenges — predicting 3D protein structures from amino acid sequences. Has predicted structures of virtually all known proteins, transforming drug discovery.
2021 · Nobel Prize categoryNature
REASONING
DeepSeek-R1: Incentivizing Reasoning via RL
DeepSeek-AI Research Team
arXiv, 2025
Pure RL approach to developing reasoning capabilities without supervised fine-tuning data. Matches OpenAI o1 on math and coding benchmarks at a fraction of the training cost.
2025 · DeepSeek R1arXiv
VISION
An Image is Worth 16x16 Words (ViT)
Dosovitskiy, Beyer et al. — Google Brain
ICLR 2021
Applied Transformer architecture directly to image patches, challenging CNN dominance. Foundation for all modern multimodal AI, visual reasoning, and computer vision systems.
2021 · ViT foundationarXiv
ALIGNMENT
Constitutional AI: Harmlessness from AI Feedback
Bai, Jones, Ndousse et al. — Anthropic
arXiv, 2022
Training approach where AI models critique their own outputs using written principles — enabling scalable AI safety without large amounts of human feedback data. Foundation of Claude.
2022 · Claude foundationarXiv
ROBOTICS
RT-2: Vision-Language-Action Models
Brohan, Brown et al. — Google DeepMind
CoRL 2023
Vision-language models trained on web data applied directly to robotic control. Robots reason about novel tasks from text descriptions — the dawn of general-purpose physical AI.
2023 · Physical AIarXiv
QUANTUM
Quantum Supremacy Using a Superconducting Processor
Arute, Arya et al. — Google Quantum AI
Nature, 2019
Google's Sycamore processor computed in 200 seconds what would take the best classical supercomputer 10,000 years — the first experimental demonstration of quantum supremacy.
2019 · Quantum leapNature
RL
Mastering Go with Deep Neural Networks
Silver, Huang et al. — DeepMind
Nature, 2016
AlphaGo became the first AI to defeat a world champion Go player. Deep RL + Monte Carlo tree search conquered a task long thought impossible for machines, changing AI forever.
2016 · AlphaGoNature
