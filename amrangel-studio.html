// The Atelier System

One studio, every step of the garment.

Each tool is built on the same 3D core — so a pattern you cut becomes cloth that drapes, and cloth that drapes becomes a file you can send to production.

01

Sewing Machine Simulator

Operate a fully animated industrial machine — needle, feed dog, bobbin, and tension — and learn the craft by doing it.

02

Cloth Physics Engine

Cotton, silk, denim, and leather each fall differently. Real weight, stretch, and wrinkle behavior on every avatar pose.

03

Living Avatars

Adjustable bodies down to the centimeter — chest, waist, sleeve, inseam — with a full pose and catwalk library.

04

Pattern Generator

Front, back, sleeve, and collar pieces, graded across sizes, with seam allowance and cutting layout — export to PDF or DXF.

05

AI Design Assistant

Describe a garment in plain language. Get a pattern, fabric pairing, palette, accessories, and a cost and time estimate.

06

Tailoring Academy

Beginner to professional lessons with 3D simulations, quizzes, and certificates — track your progress as you go.

// From Sketch to Sample

A workflow that mirrors the real atelier.

Cut

Draft the pattern

Draw, cut, mirror, and grade pieces directly in 3D space, with snap guides and live seam allowance.

Drape

Simulate the fabric

Choose a material and watch it fall, stretch, and wrinkle on the avatar under real gravity and wind.

Stitch

Run the machine

Feed the panel through the simulator — needle penetration, thread tension, and stitch formation, modeled stitch by stitch.

Ship

Export production files

Send finished garments to PDF, DXF, GLB, or OBJ — ready for a pattern maker or a game engine alike.
