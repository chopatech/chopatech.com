The flow, in order

From ceremony to album, in three moments.

No account, no download. Guests join with a code they already have in hand — the one on the table card, the program, or the screen at the door.

01 — Arrive

Scan or type the code

A QR code on the invitation or table card opens the upload page instantly — no download, no login screen standing between the guest and the photo.

02 — Capture

Shoot, drop, or select

Guests take a photo directly, or drag in a batch from their camera roll. Everything compresses and uploads in the background, even on patchy venue wifi.

03 — Curate

The organizer shapes the album

Blurry shots and duplicates are flagged automatically. The organizer approves, features, and shares the final gallery — or exports it in full.
